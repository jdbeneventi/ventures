üéØ Core Philosophy

Since covering overhead isn't an issue and this is part-time:

  • Optimize for learning, not revenue
  • Build sustainable rhythms, not burnout
  • Quality over speed
  • Community trust over quick scale

üóìÔ∏è Four-Phase Rollout (12-18 Months)

Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-12)

Status: Accelerated Track - Can launch in 2-3 weeks!

Goal: 10-15 founding members, prove concept

Key Activities:

  • Complete systems setup (Pike13, Shopify, legal entity formation)
  • Recruit 2-3 facilitators for core classes
  • Soft launch with invitation-only founding members
  • Validate programming and pricing
  • Build operational rhythms

Success Metric: 10-15 committed founding members, positive feedback on programming

Phase 2: Validation (Weeks 13-24)

Goal: 25-35 members, regular programming established

Key Activities:

  • Public launch and marketing push
  • Expand to 6 daytime classes per week
  • Add 2 night events per week
  • Launch Red Tent Hour
  • Optimize pricing and offerings based on data

Success Metric: 25-35 active members, 80%+ retention, break-even achieved

Phase 3: Optimization (Weeks 25-52)

Goal: 50-70 members, wholesale pilot launched

Key Activities:

  • Mature operations with consistent quality
  • Pilot wholesale elixir program (3-5 accounts)
  • Expand night events to 3-4x per week
  • Hire 1-2 part-time facilitators or space managers
  • Build Ethos member pipeline

Success Metric: 50-70 members, $15k+ monthly net profit, 3-5 wholesale accounts

Phase 4: Scale (Months 13-18)

Goal: 80-100 members, decision point on hiring help

Key Activities:

  • Decision: Hire part-time manager or stay lean?
  • Expand wholesale to 5-8 accounts
  • Launch e-commerce platform for broader reach
  • Optimize for Joshua/Sarah to work less, earn more
  • Evaluate integration opportunities with Ethos

Success Metric: 80-100 members, $28-35k monthly net profit, sustainable part-time model

‚ö†Ô∏è Risk Mitigation

Risk: Slow Member Growth

Mitigation:

  • Monthly check-ins
  • Exit options at milestones

Mitigation:

  • Start with Sarah's restaurant connections
  • Delay until Phase 3 (after operations stable)
  • Simple fulfillment model initially
